Coming soon · Founding cycle

Research

Our founding research cycle is underway. The first findings — on how AI is genuinely used across venture capital, private equity, investment banking, and wealth management — will publish here.

We won't publish until we have something real to say. No borrowed statistics, no recycled hype — just first-hand research from conversations with people doing the work. That takes a little time, and it's worth the wait.

The research agenda

  1. 01

    Efficient uses

    Where AI genuinely creates an edge in research, diligence, and dealmaking — and how the best teams actually capture it.

  2. 02

    Overhyped bets

    Where money and attention are flowing into AI that doesn't hold up — the tools, claims, and workflows that underdeliver.

  3. 03

    Real workflows

    How investors and bankers use AI day to day in practice — the unglamorous, concrete adoption behind the headlines.

Eight research tracks

Each cohort member owns one track.

Every track is grounded in primary research — you'll reach out to and learn from professionals and interns at the firms you study, then report what's really working and what isn't.

  • 01

    Deal sourcing & origination

    Is AI finding better deals — or just more noise?

  • 02

    Due diligence & document review

    Where data-room AI saves hours vs. where it can't.

  • 03

    Financial modeling & analysis

    What juniors actually use vs. where it breaks.

  • 04

    Investment & market research

    Differentiated insight, or commoditized?

  • 05

    Memo, CIM & pitch generation

    First-draft speed vs. narrative and credibility.

  • 06

    Wealth management & client work

    Adoption vs. fiduciary and trust limits.

  • 07

    Risk, compliance & back office

    Where AI quietly works best — and least hyped.

  • 08

    The tooling map

    Catalog the real tools: sticky vs. vaporware.

How we research

We don't recycle hot takes. We go to the source.

  1. 01

    We talk to the people doing the work

    Interviews with analysts, associates, and partners across banking, private equity, venture, and wealth — plus direct surveys of practitioners and interns. We test the tools firms actually use, and we track what they're hiring for.

  2. 02

    We ground every claim in real data

    Industry research — McKinsey, BCG, the Evident AI Index — alongside earnings calls and company filings. Sourced and cited, not rumor.

  3. 03

    We watch where the conversation lives

    LinkedIn, Reddit, X, and practitioner newsletters tell us what to investigate next — leads we then verify at the source before we publish.
