Research

Our founding research cycle is underway. The first findings — on how AI is genuinely used across venture capital, private equity, investment banking, and wealth management — will publish here.

We won't publish until we have something real to say. No borrowed statistics, no recycled hype — just first-hand research from conversations with people doing the work. That takes a little time, and it's worth the wait.

The research agenda

  1. 01

    Efficient uses

    Where AI genuinely creates an edge in research, diligence, and dealmaking — and how the best teams actually capture it.

  2. 02

    Overhyped bets

    Where money and attention are flowing into AI that doesn't hold up — the tools, claims, and workflows that underdeliver.

  3. 03

    Real workflows

    How investors and bankers use AI day to day in practice — the unglamorous, concrete adoption behind the headlines.
