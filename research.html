How it works

You pick the question. You own it end to end.

You're not handed an assignment. You choose what you're most curious about, then carry it through two months of real research.

  1. 01

    Choose your focus

    Pick what grabs you — one of the directions below, a blend of a few, or your own angle entirely.

  2. 02

    Build your plan

    Scope your question and map a two-month research plan with the founder.

  3. 03

    Do the research

    Each week: a 300-word brief, a LinkedIn excerpt, and a short deck you present at the all-hands. You're responsible for the primary research — networking toward and landing real interviews with professionals and interns at the firms you study.

  4. 04

    Publish your findings

    Cap it with a final paper — what you gathered, what you learned, and the recommendations you'd make — plus a presentation deck you deliver to the cohort.

How we research

We don't recycle hot takes. We go to the source.

  1. 01

    We talk to the people doing the work

    Interviews with analysts, associates, and partners across banking, private equity, venture, and wealth — plus direct surveys of practitioners and interns. We test the tools firms actually use, and we track what they're hiring for.

  2. 02

    We ground every claim in real data

    Industry research — McKinsey, BCG, the Evident AI Index — alongside earnings calls and company filings. Sourced and cited, not rumor.

  3. 03

    We watch where the conversation lives

    LinkedIn, Reddit, X, and practitioner newsletters tell us what to investigate next — leads we then verify at the source before we publish.
