About the research

Honest research on how AI is really used in finance.

Every firm in finance is racing to put AI to work, and most of what's said about it is hype. We report what genuinely works, what's overhyped, and the real workflows behind it: first-hand, sourced, and never recycled.

  • We talk to the people doing the work: analysts, associates, partners, and interns.
  • We ground every claim in real data: filings, earnings calls, and industry research.
  • We watch where the conversation lives, then verify at the source before we publish.
