Most coverage of AI in finance sells excitement. We do something rarer — we report what's actually being used inside investment banking, private equity & venture capital, wealth management & financial planning, and corporate finance, and we say plainly where the hype runs ahead of reality.

Why it matters

“So — what do you think about AI in our industry?”

You're going to get asked. Most candidates freeze. Every bank, every PE firm, every VC is scrambling to figure out AI right now — and the students who can really speak to it stand out the moment the question comes. This is where you get the answer. And it doesn't stop at the interview:

  1. 01

    In recruiting

    Walk into interviews with a specific, informed point of view on how the industry actually uses AI — not a guess.

  2. 02

    On the job

    Understand how AI really shows up in the work, so you bring more to the table from day one of your internship and well beyond.

  3. 03

    On your resume

    Real research experience, published under your name — proof you can think, dig, and communicate.

Who we're looking for

Students who want the truth about AI before they enter the industry.

  • Undergraduates aiming for investment banking, private equity & venture capital, wealth management, or corporate finance
  • Genuinely curious about how AI is changing the work — not just the hype
  • Willing to network toward and land real interviews with professionals at the firms studied
  • Any year, any major. Drive matters more than a resume line.
