The short version

What the program is.

Applied Intelligence Collective is an independent, student-founded research community studying how AI is actually being used across finance. Over eight remote weeks you scope your own question inside one of four finance areas, publish a weekly brief and post, and finish with a capstone paper and deck. Strong researchers can be staffed on real, paid client work along the way.

Want the full picture?

The weekly rhythm, the four research areas, the team structure, the tools, and what you walk away with are all laid out in the program overview (PDF). Honest note: we're independent and new, not affiliated with any university, employer, or fund, and the program is unpaid.
