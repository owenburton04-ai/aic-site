The mission

We figure out how AI is really used across finance.

Applied Intelligence Collective is an independent, student-founded research community studying how AI is actually being used across finance: what genuinely works, what's overhyped, and the real workflows behind it. You research a question you care about, publish real work, and build fluency that carries into your career.

Honest about what we are.

Independent and new. We're not affiliated with any university, employer, or fund, and the program is unpaid. What you get is real research experience, a portfolio, and a network.

Where the evidence comes from

Four ways to find real evidence.

1

Conversations & calls

Ask people how they actually use AI. 20 minutes is plenty, and warm intros go far.

2

Filings & disclosures

10-Ks, earnings calls, and investor materials increasingly spell out real AI use.

3

LinkedIn, Reddit & more

Done well, these surface practitioner workflows you won't find anywhere official.

4

Whatever else works

No fixed list. If you find a good source we didn't mention, use it.
