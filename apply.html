How it works

You pick the question. You own it end to end.

You're not handed an assignment. You choose what you're most curious about, then carry it through two months of real research.

  1. 01

    Choose your focus

    Pick what grabs you — one of our research directions, a blend of a few, or your own angle entirely.

  2. 02

    Build your plan

    Scope your question and map a two-month research plan with the founder.

  3. 03

    Do the research

    Each week: a 300-word brief, a LinkedIn excerpt, and a short deck you present at the all-hands. You're responsible for the primary research — networking toward and landing real interviews with professionals and interns at the firms you study.

  4. 04

    Publish your findings

    Cap it with a final paper — what you gathered, what you learned, and the recommendations you'd make — plus a presentation deck you deliver to the cohort.

By applying you agree to be contacted about Applied Intelligence Collective. We don't share your information.
